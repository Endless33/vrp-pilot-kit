# Pilot Admission Finality Policy

## Principle

Pilot availability is determined exclusively by the VRP Program.

Participation windows may close without reopening.

## Deadline

Organizations that do not submit and begin the Pilot process before the published deadline are not eligible for admission under that intake window.

## Finality

Once a Pilot intake window is closed:

- applications are no longer accepted;
- exceptions are not granted;
- commercial offers do not reopen a closed intake;
- previous pricing and terms cannot be restored.

## Non-Retroactive Policy

A closed Pilot window cannot be reopened for individual organizations regardless of:

- company size;
- commercial value;
- strategic importance;
- late requests;
- external recommendations.

## Engineering Principle

Engineering schedules are part of the protocol governance.

Deadlines are treated as engineering boundaries rather than negotiation points.
