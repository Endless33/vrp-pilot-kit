# VRP Public Roadmap

## Purpose

This document provides a high-level roadmap for the public VRP pilot program.

The roadmap communicates the expected evolution of public documentation, validation capabilities, and pilot infrastructure.

It intentionally does not disclose confidential implementation plans for the protected VRP runtime.

---

## Scope

This roadmap applies to:

- public documentation;
- pilot program evolution;
- validation capabilities;
- evidence formats;
- deployment guidance;
- integration materials.

It does not apply to:

- protected runtime implementation;
- proprietary engineering milestones;
- confidential development schedules.

---

## Roadmap Principles

The public roadmap follows several principles.

### Engineering First

Priority is given to engineering quality, reproducibility, and observable validation rather than release frequency.

---

### Evidence-Driven Progress

Public milestones are based on completed engineering work and documented validation rather than planned implementation.

---

### Stable Public Interfaces

Public integration contracts should remain stable whenever practical.

---

### Incremental Improvement

Public documentation and tooling should evolve through small, traceable improvements.

---

## Current Focus

The current public priorities include:

- improving deployment documentation;
- expanding validation guidance;
- refining evidence specifications;
- strengthening public integration contracts;
- improving pilot onboarding materials.

---

## Future Areas

Future public work may include:

- additional deployment examples;
- expanded validation scenarios;
- improved reporting templates;
- enhanced evidence documentation;
- broader operational guidance.

Publication timing depends on engineering progress and validation results.

---

## Public Deliverables

Future public releases may include:

- updated documentation;
- revised interface contracts;
- new report templates;
- additional validation examples;
- expanded deployment references.

---

## Roadmap Limitations

The roadmap is informational only.

It should not be interpreted as:

- a contractual commitment;
- a delivery guarantee;
- a complete engineering schedule.

Priorities may change based on engineering findings and pilot feedback.

---

## Out of Scope

This roadmap intentionally excludes:

- protected runtime implementation plans;
- proprietary research activities;
- confidential product strategy;
- internal engineering milestones.

---

## Roadmap Invariants

The roadmap preserves the following principles.

- engineering quality takes precedence over release frequency;
- public milestones are evidence-driven;
- implementation remains confidential;
- public documentation evolves incrementally;
- observable behavior remains the basis for public communication.

---

## Summary

The VRP public roadmap provides a high-level view of the continued evolution of the public pilot ecosystem while maintaining the separation between publicly documented capabilities and the protected runtime implementation.
