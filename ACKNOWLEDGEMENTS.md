# Acknowledgements

## Purpose

This repository exists because of the time, feedback, technical discussions, and engineering observations contributed by many individuals throughout the development of the VRP Pilot Program.

While the protected VRP runtime remains privately developed, the public pilot ecosystem has benefited from constructive review, independent validation, and community discussion.

The project acknowledges engineers, researchers, operators, and reviewers who have contributed through:

- technical discussions;
- engineering feedback;
- deployment observations;
- documentation review;
- validation recommendations;
- reproducibility testing.

Constructive technical criticism has helped improve the clarity and quality of the public pilot materials.
